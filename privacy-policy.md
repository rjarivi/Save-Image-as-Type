Privacy Policy for Save Image As TypeLast Updated: March 17, 2026This Privacy Policy describes how "Save Image As Type" (the "Extension") handles user data. We are committed to ensuring your privacy and being transparent about how our Extension works.1. Data Collection and Usage"Save Image As Type" does not collect, store, or transmit any personal data. - All image processing (conversion between PNG, JPG, and WebP) happens locally on your machine using the browser's internal APIs.No images, URLs, or browsing history are ever sent to any external servers.2. Permissions ExplainedThe Extension requires the following permissions to function:contextMenus: To add the "Save Image As Type" option to your right-click menu.downloads: To allow the browser to save the converted image to your computer.offscreen: To create a temporary local environment for image processing (Canvas API) which is required by Manifest V3 security standards.host_permissions (<all_urls>): To access image data on websites so it can be converted. This data stays entirely within your browser.3. Third-Party ServicesWe do not use any third-party analytics, tracking, or advertising services.4. Changes to This PolicyWe may update this policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected by the "Last Updated" date at the top of this page.5. ContactIf you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact the developer via the Chrome Web Store support dashboard.
